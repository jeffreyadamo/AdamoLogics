Pandemic Pantry


Pandemic Pantry will search recipies using the Spoonacular API and only display the ingredients, instructions, picture, and link to the source page. No ads or 5 pages of extra fluff! Searches can be adjusted to your diet type or specific food intolerances. It was my first group project during bootcamp and even though it's simple, I use it all the time!
