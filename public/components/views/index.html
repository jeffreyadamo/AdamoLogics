Recipe Summarizer

Recipe Summarizer will search recipies using the Spoonacular API and only display the important stuff:
  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • A picture
  • Link to the source page

No ads or 5 pages of extra fluff.
Searches can be adjusted to your diet type or specific food intolerances.
This was from my first group project during bootcamp and even though it's simple, I use it all the time! Enjoy.
